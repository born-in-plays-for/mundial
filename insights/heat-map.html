Talent-Production Intensity

Not "where were the most players born" — a raw density map like that would just track megacity population. This asks a different question: does a place produce more World Cup 2026 talent than its population alone would predict? Purple means well above the global average, teal means below it, pale means roughly what you'd expect — normalised by population, not a raw player count, which is why the result isn't simply dominated by the biggest cities.
