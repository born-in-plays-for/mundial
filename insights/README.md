- Instagram portrait 4:5 (1080×1350) gets the most feed real estate — preferred for single-image posts
- LinkedIn landscape 1.91:1 (1200×627) is the standard for link previews and article posts
- Facebook accepts the same ratios as LinkedIn for feed posts
- All three platforms display square (1:1) cleanly as a safe universal fallback

For a composite like your before/after, LinkedIn landscape 1200×627 or Instagram square 1080×1080 would be the natural fits.
